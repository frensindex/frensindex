🐺 FRENS Index

A crowd driven legitimacy filter for crypto.

FRENS started as a simple signal engine. The idea was straightforward. Let people swipe through tokens and label them as fren or rug.

What it turned into is something much more interesting.

Instead of trying to predict price, FRENS captures sentiment. Not from influencers or insiders, but from participation. Every swipe contributes to a shared signal. Over time, that signal becomes a public layer of reputation for tokens.

No wallets required. No friction. Just open the app and start swiping.

⸻

⚙️ How it works
	•	Tokens are pulled live from Dexscreener
	•	Each project becomes a swipeable card
	•	Users signal:
	•	Fren → looks legit
	•	Rug → something feels off
	•	Signals are aggregated into a Pack Score
	•	The score reflects crowd conviction, not hype

The system is designed to be fast, simple, and addictive.

⸻

🧠 Design philosophy

This was built around a few core ideas:

1. Participation over prediction
Nobody knows what will pump. But people are very good at spotting weird behavior.

2. Shared state matters
Local opinions are useless. Aggregated opinions become signal.

3. Friction kills usage
No wallets. No forms. No barriers. Just swipe.

4. Stability is a feature
The system favors consistency over complexity. Simple flows that always work beat advanced features that break.

⸻

🔍 Discovery engine

The discovery system pulls in live tokens and filters them in real time based on:
	•	liquidity
	•	volume
	•	recency
	•	basic sanity checks

Projects are continuously fed into the stack so the experience feels endless.

As you swipe, more tokens load in the background.

⸻

👤 Profiles and reputation

When signed in:
	•	Your signals are tracked
	•	Your participation builds reputation
	•	Your activity contributes to the public dataset

Reputation starts with participation. Accuracy comes later.

⸻

🚧 Current state

The core loop is live:
	•	discovery
	•	swipe signaling
	•	shared scoring
	•	leaderboard
	•	profiles

You can use it today.

What is not live yet:
	•	paid signal expansions

Those are coming next.

⸻

🔮 Where this is going

FRENS is moving toward becoming a public legitimacy layer for crypto.

A place where:
	•	new tokens are filtered by real users
	•	patterns emerge from behavior
	•	reputation becomes visible

Not replacing charts. Not replacing research.

Just answering one simple question:

does this feel real or not?
